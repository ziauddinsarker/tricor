Board of Directors

Michael Roberts, Non-Executive Chairman

Mr. Michael Charles Roberts is Non-Executive Chairman of the Board of the Company. Mr. Roberts is currently a director of Obbard Ltd, a property investment and development company with offices in London and Hong Kong. Mr. Roberts is primarily responsible for leading its Asian business development activities and partnerships with investors in the Far East. Prior to Obbard Ltd, Mr. Roberts held various senior management positions with Standard Chartered Bank based in Singapore and Jakarta for 6 years. Whilst there, Mr. Roberts was the Head of Corporate Restructuring for Southeast Asia where he led a team to develop financial solutions to resolve and restructure corporate loans with major conglomerates and corporations during the Asian Financial Crisis between 1997 and 1999. Prior to that, he was Head of Corporate Banking and Deputy Country Head of Standard Chartered Bank in Indonesia. Between 1989 and 1994, he worked for Westpac Banking Corporation for 5 years, where he held senior positions as Head of Regional Audit and Head of Banking & Credit in their Hong Kong and Jakarta offices. Mr. Roberts started his career with National Westminster Bank in London from 1981 to 1985 and then moved to Continental Bank as a manager in audit and credit review in their London and Hong Kong offices. Mr. Roberts has a B.Sc. (Hons) from Durham University and is a Member of the Institute of Directors (MIoD).

Chan Meng Fook, CEO and Executive

Mr. Chan obtained a law degree from the National University of Singapore in 1985 and was called to the Singapore Bar on 12 February 1986. Since then he has practiced as an advocate and Mr solicitor in Singapore. Chan is one of the founders, and was a director, of LLC, a Singapore law UniLegal corporation. In the course of his work, Mr. Chan has handled, and been involved in, a number of corporate finance matters and various mergers and acquisitions. Mr. Chan has also dealt with issues relating to the rescue and restructuring of various companies and their acquisition of businesses

Morgan, Christopher Non-Executive Director

Mr. Christopher Andrew Morgan has been appointed as Non-Executive Director of Tricor plc., effective November 15, 2013. Chris has gained extensive experience in commercial investigations during his career, and more recently has focussed on investment advisory and management. He has previously served with the Metropolitan Police and the National Criminal Intelligence Service where he received a number of Commendations. Chris currently sits on the Board of a number of investment companies, including AIM quoted Nova Resources Ltd.
