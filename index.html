Welcome to Tricor

Tricor Plc (AIM: TRIC) is an investment company focussed on the natural resources sector and is listed on the Alternative Investment Market (AIM) of the London Stock Exchange.

In line with Tricor's investing policy, the Company aims to build a diverse investment portfolio, which will provide exposure to a number of commodities and projects, whilst mitigating risk for the Company's shareholders.

The Board of Tricor Plc are continuously seeking further investment opportunities which will bring further value to the investment portfolio and enhance long term shareholder value.
